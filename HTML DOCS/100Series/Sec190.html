SECTION 190 - SUPPLY AND TRANSPORT OF ROADMAKING MATERIALS

190.01 DESCRIPTION

This section covers the requirements for the supply and transport of roadmaking materials such as filling, pavement materials, aggregates, bitumen products and topsoil.

Supply includes loading of material into vehicles.

190.04 STOCKPILING OF DELIVERED MATERIAL

Where delivery of material into stockpile is specified, clearing and preparation of stockpile sites will be done by VicRoads unless otherwise specified.

Stockpiles shall be so located and constructed that they do not unduly reduce sight distance at locations such as intersections and curves. Stockpiles shall not be located under or immediately adjacent to overhead electric power lines or elsewhere where the overhead clearance from the surface for dumping is less than 6 m. Stockpiles shall be located clear of the road formation, drains, entrances, trees and side tracks and the toes of the stockpiles shall not impede the operation of mechanical loading equipment.

Where material is supplied from more than one source, material from each source shall be stockpiled separately.

All stockpiles shall be constructed in such a way that segregation of the material is minimised.
